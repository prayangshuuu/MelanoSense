Bridging the gap in
Dermatological Care

Melanoma is a severe form of skin cancer that can be life-threatening if not caught early. In regions like Bangladesh, access to specialized dermatologists can be limited, leading to delayed diagnoses.

MelanoSense provides a fast, accessible, and AI-driven preliminary screening to help users understand their risk levels.
