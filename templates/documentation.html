About the Project

MelanoSense is a student-led AI healthcare initiative focused on early melanoma awareness and AI-based pre-screening in Bangladesh. Our mission is to provide an accessible, fast, and reliable decision-support tool for everyone, especially in areas with limited dermatological access.

How the AI Works

CNN Image Model

Our Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) analyzes pixel-level details of skin lesions to identify malignant patterns that might be invisible to the naked eye.

Clinical Metadata Model

We integrate clinical factors like age, sex, and localization using an XGBoost model, providing a more holistic risk assessment.

Explainable Heatmaps

Using Grad-CAM technology, we generate heatmaps that highlight exactly which areas of the image the AI found suspicious.

Intended Use & Limitations

MelanoSense is designed for **decision support only**. It is NOT a replacement for professional medical diagnosis. The AI's confidence levels are probabilistic and should be reviewed by a qualified doctor.

Privacy & Data Protection

We take user privacy seriously. All uploaded images and clinical data are encrypted and stored securely. We do not share individual data with third parties without explicit consent.
